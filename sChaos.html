  • Chaos Game The Chaos game gives a method for generating arbitrarily close approximations to the Sierpinski triangle. It can be described with the following algorithm:

    1 Begin with three vertices and randomly choose a point X inside the triangle they form.

    2 Randomly choose one of the three vertices and draw an imaginary line between X and our random vertex.

    3 Plot a dot on the midpoint Y of this line.

    4 Let X=Y and go to step 2. Repeat an arbitrarily large number of times.
