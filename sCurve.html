  • Sierpinski Curve The Sierpinski arrowhead curve is a base-motif fractal identical to the Sierpinski triangle for arbitrarily large numbers of iterations. Notice that the length of the one-dimensional curve that forms this fractal is increased by a factor of 3/2 with each iteration. Thus, although it is identical in area and Hausdorff dimension to the Sierpinski triangle, the limit of the length of the curve is infinity.
